Svein Tuft's Story

More than 20 years ago I was exploring by bike as a young man in Northern Canada, Yukon and Alaska. In a strange turn of events this led me to my profession of 20 years in the pro peleton. I was riding gravel before it was cool. In those days it was what you had to do to get up North. I loved it then but I love it more now. The bikes and technology have been a big factor in that.
